Perak: Land of Heritage and Nature

Perak is a diverse and vibrant state located on the west coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and stunning natural landscapes, Perak offers something for every traveler. The capital city, Ipoh, charms visitors with its colonial architecture, local cuisine, and street art. Nature lovers can explore attractions like Gua Tempurung, lush rainforests, and scenic waterfalls. Gua Tempurung

Gua Tempurung is one of the largest limestone caves in Peninsular Malaysia, located in Perak. It stretches over 4 kilometers and features stunning rock formations, underground rivers, and spacious chambers. Visitors can explore the cave through guided tours that range from easy walks to adventurous river crawls, making it a favorite spot for nature lovers and thrill-seekers.
