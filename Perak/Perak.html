Perak: Land of Heritage and Nature

Perak is a diverse and vibrant state located on the west coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and stunning natural landscapes, Perak offers something for every traveler. The capital city, Ipoh, charms visitors with its colonial architecture, local cuisine, and street art. Nature lovers can explore attractions like Gua Tempurung, lush rainforests, and scenic waterfalls.
