Alor Setar Tower: A Symbol of Kedah's Skyline

Alor Setar Tower, or Menara Alor Setar, is a prominent landmark in Kedah's capital city. Standing at 165.5 meters, it serves as both a telecommunications tower and a tourist attraction. Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of Alor Setar and even the Langkawi islands from its observation deck.
