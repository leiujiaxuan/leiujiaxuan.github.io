Top 3 Places to Visit in Negeri Sembilan

1. Port Dickson Beach

Port Dickson is one of the most popular beach destinations on the west coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Known for its long stretch of sandy beaches, such as Pantai Teluk Kemang and Pantai Cahaya Negeri, it offers a perfect weekend escape for families, couples, and beach lovers. Visitors can enjoy water sports, beachside resorts, seafood dining, and beautiful sunsets.

2. Centipede Temple (Then Sze Koon Temple)

Located atop Bukit Jong in Seremban, Centipede Temple is a well-known Taoist temple offering scenic hilltop views and a peaceful atmosphere. Visitors climb 264 steps to reach the temple, which features intricate Chinese architecture, statues of deities, and a colorful pagoda. Named after the centipedes once found around the area, itâ€™s a popular spot for cultural exploration and spiritual blessings, especially among couples.

3. Jeram Toi Waterfall

Nestled in the lush tropical rainforest between Seremban and Kuala Kelawang, Jeram Toi is a scenic waterfall and recreational spot perfect for nature lovers. It's a popular weekend destination for picnics, hiking, and swimming in natural pools. The area is well-maintained with walking trails and basic amenities, making it suitable for families and casual hikers.
