1. Batu Caves

Batu Caves is a famous limestone hill featuring a series of caves and Hindu temples, located just outside Kuala Lumpur. Dominated by a towering golden statue of Lord Murugan, the site is accessed by climbing 272 colorful steps. It is especially popular during the annual Thaipusam festival and is a major spiritual and tourist attraction.

2. Sunway Lagoon

Sunway Lagoon is a sprawling theme park located in Bandar Sunway, offering over 90 attractions across water parks, amusement rides, extreme sports, wildlife zones, and scream-inducing haunted houses. It's a family-friendly destination perfect for a fun-filled day of thrills and entertainment.

3. i-City Shah Alam

i-City is a digital city located in Shah Alam, best known for its vibrant LED-lit nightscapes and themed attractions. Visitors can enjoy the Snowalk ice house, water park, 3D trick art museum, and more. It's especially popular at night and caters to both local and international tourists.
