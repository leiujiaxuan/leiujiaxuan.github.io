Top 3 Places in Johor

Sultan Abu Bakar Mosque

1. Sultan Abu Bakar Mosque

Sultan Abu Bakar State Mosque is the state mosque of Johor, constructed between 1892 and 1900. The mosque is set out to recreate colonial English Victorian architecture. The mosque features some Moorish architecture elements along with some Malay influence.

Desaru

2. Desaru

Desaru offers a wide range of attractions, from its pristine beaches and thrilling water activities to fun-filled experiences at the waterpark and mini golf. With so much to explore, Desaru promises an unforgettable experience.

Kota Tinggi Firefly Park

3. Kota Tinggi Firefly Park

Kota Tinggi Firefly Park offers a magical experience with thousands of fireflies lighting up the mangroves along the Johor River. You can enjoy serene night time boat tours while looking at beautiful glowing fireflies, and learn facts about fireflies.
